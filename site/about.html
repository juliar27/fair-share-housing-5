About Us

mapFSH

mapFSH (Map Fair Share Housing) is a project taken on by The Fair Share Housing Center of New Jersey to make their data regarding available residential properties within the state of NJ open-source.

The Fair Share Housing Center of New Jersey is a non-for-profit organization that fights for the rights of low-income communities to get their "fair share" of the community. The Fair Share Housing works to end any and all discriminatory practices against low-income home seekers in the state of New Jersey through legislation.
